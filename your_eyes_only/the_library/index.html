Ways of Being
My thoughts

Uplifting in its celebration of life (its diversity, its interconnectivity), Ways of Being is one of the few nonfiction books I can recommend to improve your mood and optimism about the world.

In this book, James Bridle asks the ambitious question, "What is intelligence?", and responds to it in great depth from multiple perspectives, including biology, language, art history, anthropology, and technology.
