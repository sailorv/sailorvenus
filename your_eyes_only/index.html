we thought the internet would solve loneliness. as technology advanced, we thought it might bridge the gap between people - between languages. perhaps one day, we thought, it would even bridge the gap between species.

in early twenty-twenty, the limits of that theory were tested. people were thrust into complete isolation. and what we saw was that technology didn't make us less lonely - but perhaps even more lonely than before. as conspiracy proliferated, people lost empathy, connection, and a reason to care for others. instead, people used any form of communication they had to argue, to fight. all of the good that technology had accomplished was undone, and people are loneliner now than ever.

***

the internet used to be cool. it used to be a place you could go to experience and see things you couldn't see anywhere else. to meet people you would never encounter in real life - freaks driven to the fringes of social circles, eager to meet nerds just like themselves.

now it's just a place to go to buy things. it's deeply unsexy - the color analysis, nervous system regulation, thinly-veiled mlm schemes. the internet isn't weird anymore.

your_eyes_only is a fashion collection, but it's also a place on the internet to get away from the bullshit.

Frequently Asked Questions

Who are you?

My name is Catherine, and I'm an anime fan and fashion otaku who lives in Texas.

How did you build this site?

I'm a web developer.

How did you learn to sew?

My mom taught me how to sew many years ago. I got my start making cosplay costumes for anime conventions, and didn't sew for many years later, until I was in college. Then I used sewing to make art installations while I was studying fine art.

Do you have any other websites?

Yes, sailorven.us and milk-experiment.net.

Do you plan to sell the clothing you make?

No, it's just a hobby. There are so many other amazing designers to support, and too many clothes in the world right now.
