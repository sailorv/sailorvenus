april 8, 2024

when sunlight becomes daylight in a single minute. when the stars and planets fade into view, and i realize they've been above us all day. the birds stop singing, suddenly, in reverence to the thing that has brought crowds of people into the parks in the middle of a monday.

among the most haunting events i've witnessed: the total solar eclispe over texas in 2024.

as the moon began transiting the sun, daylight dimmed. my friends and i split a bottle of wine on a patio - all the more enjoyable because none of us were at work, but participating in spooky, prehistoric revelry.

during a new moon, a solar eclipse may occur if the moon and the sun are aligned just right along the ecliptic. the more precisely the moon aligns with the sun's path, the more "total" the eclipse.

that our moon's apparent size is almost exactly that of our sun is one of the greatest cosmic wonders to me. it's as if our solar system gifted us this beautiful phenomenon, knowing we are only planet that can gaze up from our surface and appreciate it.
