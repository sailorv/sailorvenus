Death to social media

Death to social media.
Death to likes and comments. Sign or view a guestbook. Join a webring.
Death to dms. Send a Hotmail.

Death to algorithms.
Death to showing you “what you want to see”, to showing you what they want you to buy.
Death to deciding for you.
Death to deciding for you, based on a very limited archetype, who you are, and what you want to see.
Death to sponsored posts. Long live pop-ups and banners.

Death to Analytics. Death to Insights. Death to insight. Long live site counters.

Death to link in bio. Your site is your bio, and it's stamped with gifs and low-res jpegs. What are your favorite links? The non-transactional kind?

Death to personal brands. Show us your status. Tell us your Livejournal mood. Are you awake? Indescribable? Quixotic?

Death to Google Pay, Apple Pay. Visit your post office, purchase a money order, put it in an envelope, lick it, stamp it, address it.
